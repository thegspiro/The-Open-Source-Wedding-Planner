Tips for Seating Preferences

Couples & Partners: Always seat together. Plus-ones are grouped automatically.
Exes & Conflicts: Mark "apart" with high priority (8-10) to keep on separate tables.
Families: Use household groups on guest profiles to auto-group. Or add "together" preferences.
Friend Groups: Mark key connectors as "together" and the algorithm will cluster their friends nearby.
